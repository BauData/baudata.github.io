Sky Gradients

March 2017

Why am I here? A sense of individual autonomy brings me to depict natural sceneries. Traveling around the world, I randomly choose a city to explore and understand. The use of mathematical functions allows me to depict the sky, focusing on the variation of light as center of the composition. My color palette depends on the city weather and my creations come to challenge human perceptions.
